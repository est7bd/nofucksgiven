I'm Darrell. Los Angeles.

This site isn't a portfolio. It isn't a brand play. It isn't designed to make me look good for potential investors or clients or anyone else who might Google me. Those sites exist elsewhere. This isn't one of them.

This is where I say what I actually think. About the state of this country. About MAGA theater. About the erosion of basic accountability. About the specific flavors of bullshit I watch people perform on social media every single day while the actual problems get worse.

I'm an asshole about it. I have been told this repeatedly. I lack tact — not because I don't know how to use it, but because I've decided it's not worth the effort in certain conversations. If you came here looking for nuance and gentle phrasing, you took a wrong turn somewhere.

I spent years being the person in the room who held their tongue so other people would be comfortable. Done with that. If something is stupid, I'm going to say it's stupid. If someone is lying, I'm going to say they're lying. If a movement has devolved into a personality cult, I'm going to describe it as a personality cult.

You don't have to agree. You don't even have to read this. But this is here. It's honest. And it's not going anywhere.
